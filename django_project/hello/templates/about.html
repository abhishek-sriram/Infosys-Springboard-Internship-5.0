About Human Stress Detection

Empowering you to manage your stress and lead a healthier life with advanced monitoring systems.

Key Health Parameters:

  • Snoring Rate: Indicates sleep quality and potential stress disturbances.
  • Respiratory Rate: Helps detect stress-induced rapid breathing.
  • Body Temperature: Tracks stress-related changes in body heat.
  • Limb Movement: Identifies restless behaviors during sleep.
  • Blood Oxygen Level: Ensures optimal oxygen levels during rest.
  • Eye Movements: Monitors REM sleep for stress evaluation.
  • Sleep Hours: Highlights the impact of sleep on stress levels.
  • Heart Rate: Detects stress-induced cardiac changes.
